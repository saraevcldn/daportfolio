Interactive Sales Dashboard in Microsoft Excel

Project Overview

Developed a comprehensive sales dashboard using Microsoft Excel to analyze and visualize sales performance metrics.

Problem:

    Velvetica Beauty Emporium Inc., a skincare product distribution company established in 2019, has been expanding its sales across various regions in the United States. As a startup, Velvetica lacks a data management system to effectively track and analyze their sales.

Objective:

    To create a comprehensive tracking file that:
  • Captures daily sales order transactions from January 2020 onwards.
  • Includes master files for customer details, product details, and sales team information.
  • Analyzes three years of transaction data (January 2020 to December 2022) to uncover sales trends, peak seasons, and key performance metrics.
  • Evaluates sales team performance and product sales volumes to optimize stocking and identify opportunities for growth.

    • Solution:

      As a data analyst, I developed an Excel-based interactive sales dashboard that includes:
  • A detailed tracking file for daily sales order transactions.
  • Master files containing customer, product, and sales team information.
  • Analytical tools and visualizations to identify trends, peak sales months, regional and product contributions to sales, and sales team performance.
  • Insights into the highest-selling products to ensure proper inventory management.
  • Opportunities for business improvement based on data-driven analysis.

    • Key Features

  • Interactive Charts: Dynamic charts that update with user inputs.
  • Filter Options: Slicer to filter data by year, region and product category.
  • Data Visualization: Visual representations of sales data for easy interpretation.

    • Tools & Techniques

  • Excel PivotTables and PivotCharts
  • Data validation and conditional formatting
  • Formulas and Functions: (VLOOKUP,SUMIF/S)
  • Slicers for interactive filtering

    Insights

    In analyzing the sales data, key findings were identified that provide valuable insights into the company's performance and areas for improvement. Below is a detailed summary of these insights:

    Focus Area Observation Insight
    Sales Trends Consistent increase in sales from January 2020 to December 2022, with monthly spikes The company is experiencing overall growth with seasonal peaks indicating higher sales in certain months
    Peak Seasons Sales peak in November and December each yea The holiday season significantly boosts sales, indicating strong consumer demand during these months
    Regional Performance West and Northeast regions have the highest sales volumes These regions are key markets, indicating higher brand penetration and consumer demand
    Product Contributions "Velvetica Glow Serum" and "HydraMax Moisturizer" are top-selling products These products are major revenue drivers, likely indicating high customer satisfaction and brand loyalty
    Sales Team Performance West region sales team consistently meets/exceeds goals; Midwest team falls short Disparities in sales team performance need addressing, particularly in the Midwest region
    Stocking and Inventory "Velvetica Glow Serum" frequently runs out of stock during peak months Better inventory management is needed to avoid stockouts and capitalize on high demand periods
    Opportunities for Improvement Sales growth is slower in Southeast and Midwest regions These regions present opportunities for targeted marketing and sales strategies to boost growth

    Recommendations

    Based on these insights, here are the propose strategic recommendations:

    Recommendation Details
    Enhance Inventory Management Implement advanced inventory management to ensure sufficient stock levels, especially for top-selling products during peak seasons. Utilize sales forecasts to adjust stock levels.
    Optimize Marketing Strategies ocus marketing on Southeast and Midwest regions with region-specific promotions to attract new customers.
    Support Underperforming Sales Teams Provide training and resources to the Midwest sales team, implement best practices from high-performing regions.
    Take Advantage of Peak Seasons Plan special promotions and product launches in November and December to maximize revenue. Consider bundling popular products.
    Expand Product Line Introduce new products or variations of best-sellers based on market research to maintain consumer interest and meet market demands.
    Customer Retention Programs Develop loyalty programs and personalized marketing to retain existing customers, using data to create targeted offers and incentives.
    Data-Driven Decision Making Continuously analyze sales data for emerging trends, conduct regular performance reviews, and use insights for strategic planning.
